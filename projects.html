Snake Game| Python, PyGame

Using Python and PyGame, I was able to create a game imitating the infamous Chrome "Snake Game"

Challenges Faced

  • Challenge 1: Snake Growth Mechanism:

    • Enabling the snake to grow in length when consuming the apple required careful consideration of the game's logic and structure. Managing the snake's growth without disrupting the game flow was a significant challenge.

    Resolution: After analyzing different approaches, I successfully implemented a mechanism to increase the snake's length upon apple consumption. This involved manipulating the data structures representing the snake's body and updating the display accordingly.

  • Challenge 2: Random Apple Placement:

    • A fundamental aspect of the Snake Game was ensuring that the apple appeared in random positions after the last had been consumed by the snake. Coordinating this randomness while maintaining the game's integrity presented a coding challenge.

    Resolution: After analyzing different approaches, I successfully implemented a mechanism to increase the snake's length upon apple consumption. This involved manipulating the data structures representing the snake's body and updating the display accordingly.
